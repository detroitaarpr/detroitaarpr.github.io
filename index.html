Detroit Alliance Against Racist and Political Repression

Who We Are

We are a branch of the National Alliance Against Racist and Political Repression dedicated to getting justice for victims of police crimes, establishing community control of the police in the city of Detroit, and fighting racist and political repression wherever we find it. The Detroit Police Department have been surveilling, harassing, beating, and killing us, and for what? Not to keep us safe. Their job is to protect the interests and property of white billionaires like Dan Gilbert and the Illitches, to maintain downtown as a playground for suburbanites, and to be attack dogs and bodyguards for the fat cat real estate developers and landlords that are gentrifying our neighborhoods for profit. We need to take back control of our city and put the power in the hands of the community—we need to have control of who is policing our communities, what they are allowed to do, and how they are disciplined when they cause harm to our friends and neighbors. If you want to make this a reality, we'd like to hear from you.

Where to Find Us

We meet every other Friday at 6PM at 27th Letter Books on Michigan Avenue in Detroit; please consider stopping by to learn about our work and give your input! To see what we do on the day-to-day, please consider following us on Instagram, and if you have any questions for us, please shoot us an email at detroitaarpr@proton.me.
